About Sai Darbar Hostel

Sai Darbar Telugu Boys Hostel is a dedicated accommodation facility located in Sector 20, Kopar Khairane, Navi Mumbai, catering specifically to Telugu-speaking male students and professionals. Situated in a well-connected area, the hostel offers a homely environment with amenities tailored to meet the needs of its residents.
